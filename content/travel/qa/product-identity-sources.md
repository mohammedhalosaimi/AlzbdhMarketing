# Product Identity Sources Used

This pack uses repository identity/voice rules from:

- `docs/marketing/README.md`: canonical social launch brief, claim safety, calm honest Saudi-dialect direction.
- `docs/marketing/social-post-template.md`: friction -> gentle invite -> outcome loop, no fake urgency, Western digits rule for marketing copy.
- `docs/marketing/ad-framework.md`: calm AI concierge, not a booking engine; lead with free; show real trips/places.
- `docs/design/AlzbdhDesignSystemV3.html`: Changa font, forest `#1F3D2E`, cream `#FDFBF7`, direct brand vocabulary: صراحة، يستاهل، حواليك، يلا، نبذة، الزبدة.
- `docs/BRD.md`: Arabic-first, opinionated Saudi-friend positioning across product lines.
- `.agents/skills/*`: reviewed. Available repo-local skills are engineering workflow skills (`ship`, `test-e2e`, `write-e2e`, `backlog`); none are a marketing-generation skill, so they were used only as workflow context, not as asset-generation rules.

Claim guardrails applied:

- No booking/reservation claim.
- No Android/offline claim.
- No fake urgency or fake superlatives.
- Web CTA uses `app.alzbdh.com`.
