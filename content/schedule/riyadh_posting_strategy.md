# Riyadh Posting Strategy

Date created: 2026-06-13
Timezone: Asia/Riyadh

## Recommended Windows

- X: 12:30-13:15 Riyadh time.
- Instagram: 20:30-21:15 Riyadh time.
- TikTok: 21:00-22:00 Riyadh time.

The working cadence is one theme per day, posted to all three platforms with platform-specific copy and asset sizes. X goes first around lunch to catch workday scrolling. Instagram and TikTok go in the evening, staggered 45 minutes apart so the first engagement window is cleaner.

## Weekly Mix

- Sunday: Places, preferably Riyadh/local discovery.
- Monday: Travel, especially summer-friendly destinations.
- Tuesday: Places or perfume.
- Wednesday: Mobile app utility or chat demo.
- Thursday: Mobile app weekend decision or travel.
- Friday: Review-fatigue positioning, perfume, or premium places.
- Saturday: Light discovery post, then review performance.

Offers are intentionally excluded from this cadence until dates, availability, and validity windows are confirmed.
