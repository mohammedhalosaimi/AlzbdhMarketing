## 1. Strategic Foundation

### 1.1 Positioning Statement

> **For** Saudi travelers who are tired of fragmented, halal-unfriendly trip planning,
> **الزبدة is** the AI travel companion
> **that** plans your entire trip around your values — halal dining, honest reviews, local insights — in minutes, not days.
> **Unlike** Booking.com, TripAdvisor, or WhatsApp group chaos,
> **الزبدة** doesn't sell you anything. It plans *for* you.

### 1.2 Brand Personality for Social

| Trait | In Practice |
|-------|-------------|
| **صاحبك الرحالة** (Your traveler friend) | Speak like a friend who's been everywhere, not a brand. First person plural. |
| **صريح** (Blunt-honest) | Never oversell. The زبده gists are honest — our ads should be too. |
| **Calm confidence** | No urgency tactics (this IS the differentiator vs. Booking.com's panic design). |
| **Bilingual-natural** | Arabic-first, English where it flows naturally. Code-switching like real Saudis do. |
| **Visual minimalism** | Sand tones, clean type, generous whitespace. Never cluttered. |

### 1.3 Competitive Moat Messaging

| Competitor | Their Weakness | Our Message |
|------------|---------------|-------------|
| Booking.com / Agoda | Panic-driven UI, no halal, no planning | "الزبدة ما تستعجلك. خطط براحتك." |
| TripAdvisor | Reviews without context, no itinerary | "مو بس تقييمات — خطة كاملة." |
| Google Maps | No halal verification, no trip structure | "الزبدة تعرف وش حلال ووش لا." |
| WhatsApp planning | Chaos, no output, endless voice notes | "خطة وحدة. الكل يرتاح." |
| ChatGPT / generic AI | Hallucinations, no halal data, no local knowledge | "ذكاء اصطناعي يعرف الحلال، مو يتوقعه." |

## 2. Audience Segmentation & Targeting

### 2.1 Primary Personas

#### Persona A: "أم فهد" — The Family Planner (40% of spend)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Saudi woman, 28–42, married with kids |
| **Pain** | Exhausted from planning trips that satisfy everyone. Needs halal confidence. |
| **Trigger** | School breaks, Eid holidays, summer planning |
| **Platform** | Instagram (primary), Snapchat, TikTok |
| **Hook** | "رحلة العيلة؟ خلّها علينا." |
| **Feature focus** | Halal badges, family filters, زبده gists, day-by-day itinerary |
| **Targeting** | Interests: family travel, kids activities, halal food, Turkish Airlines, Saudi tourism pages |

#### Persona B: "خالد" — The Group Organizer (25% of spend)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Saudi man, 22–35, plans trips with friends |
| **Pain** | The WhatsApp group never agrees. He ends up doing all the work. |
| **Trigger** | Long weekends, national holidays, Umrah side-trips |
| **Platform** | X/Twitter (primary), TikTok, Instagram |
| **Hook** | "خطة وحدة. الكل يرتاح." |
| **Feature focus** | Wizard speed, group-friendly itineraries, budget control |
| **Targeting** | Interests: travel deals, Saudi male lifestyle, gaming/tech crossover audience |

#### Persona C: "نورة" — The Solo Explorer (20% of spend)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Saudi woman or man, 23–32, solo or couple traveler |
| **Pain** | Wants unique experiences but doesn't know where to start. Halal is non-negotiable. |
| **Trigger** | First solo trip, honeymoon, birthday trip |
| **Platform** | TikTok (primary), Instagram |
| **Hook** | "أول رحلة لحالك؟ ما تحتاج تفكّر." |
| **Feature focus** | AI chat, destination scoring, honeymoon/solo filters |
| **Targeting** | Interests: solo travel, photography, food tourism, Japanese culture, Bali/Thailand content |

#### Persona D: "سلطان" — The Riyadh Local (15% of spend)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Riyadh resident, 20–40, looking for weekend plans |
| **Pain** | Same places every weekend. Doesn't know what's actually good. |
| **Trigger** | Thursday night, weekend boredom, date night, new restaurant openings |
| **Platform** | Instagram (primary), Snapchat, TikTok |
| **Hook** | "الويكند وصل وبعدك بالبيت؟" |
| **Feature focus** | Place discovery, Z-Score ratings, AI summaries, category filters |
| **Targeting** | Location: Riyadh. Interests: cafes, restaurants, Riyadh events, food bloggers |

### 2.2 Targeting Layers

| Layer | Parameters |
|-------|-----------|
| **Geo** | Saudi Arabia (primary), UAE/Kuwait/Bahrain (secondary — same travel behavior) |
| **Language** | Arabic (primary), Bilingual Arabic-English |
| **Age** | 22–45 core, 18–55 broad |
| **Interests** | Travel, halal food, Muslim travel, family activities, Saudi tourism, airline pages |
| **Behavioral** | Frequent travelers, travel app users, recent travel searches |
| **Lookalikes** | 1% lookalike from early adopters after Week 3 |
| **Exclusions** | Existing users (via pixel/SDK), non-Saudi residents (Phase 1 only) |

### 2.3 Retargeting Funnels

```
Funnel 1: Video Viewers → Install
├── Watched 75%+ of any Phase 1–2 reel → Serve E1/E2 install ads
├── Watched 50%+ but not 75% → Serve C1 wizard demo first, then E1
└── Watched 25%+ only → Serve A1–A4 different friction reel

Funnel 2: Installers → First Trip
├── Installed, opened, but no wizard start → F1 nudge
├── Started wizard, abandoned → Remind with F2 destination teaser
└── Completed wizard, didn't confirm trip → Retarget with during-trip C4

Funnel 3: Active → Advocate
├── Completed first trip → Ask for UGC/review
├── Generated 2+ trips → Social proof quote request
└── Shared app → Reward with exclusive destination content
```

## 4. Campaign Structure & Phasing

```
Week 1–2:  AWARENESS   → "What is الزبدة?" (reach + impressions)
                         Emotion: frustration → relief
                         Metric: CPM, video view rate

Week 3–4:  ENGAGEMENT  → "See it in action" (video views + saves)
                         Emotion: curiosity → "I want that"
                         Metric: save rate, share rate, 75% view rate

Week 5–6:  CONVERSION  → "Plan your trip — free" (app installs + first itinerary)
                         Emotion: desire → action
                         Metric: CPI, cost per first itinerary

Week 7–8:  RETENTION   → "Already planning? Here's more" (retargeting + social proof)
                         Emotion: belonging → advocacy
                         Metric: DAU, trip completion rate, UGC submissions
```

## 12. Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram
- **Reels:** 8–15s max. Arabic text overlay. Lo-fi or trending audio. Vertical 9:16.
- **Stories:** 3–5 frames. Swipe-up to app. Use polls ("وين رحلتك الجاية؟").
- **Carousels:** 4–6 slides. Save-worthy content. Educational or inspirational.
- **Feed posts:** Minimal. Brand statements only. 1:1 or 4:5 ratio.
- **Best posting times:** Thu 8–10 PM, Fri 12–2 PM, Sat 9–11 PM (Saudi peak).

### TikTok
- **Style:** Raw, authentic. UGC-style > polished. "POV: أخطط رحلة بالزبدة"
- **Hook:** First 1s must grab. Start with the friction. No brand intro.
- **Audio:** Trending sounds or calm lo-fi. Arabic voiceover when applicable.
- **Length:** 8–15s for awareness. Up to 30s for demos.
- **Hashtags:** #الزبدة #رحلة #سفر #تخطيط_رحلات #حلال #سياحة
- **Duet/Stitch bait:** Create content people want to react to — friction reels are perfect.

### X (Twitter)
- **Threads:** "خططت رحلة لإسطنبول بـ٣ دقائق — هذي القصة:" (story threads get shares)
- **Single tweets:** Punchy, relatable. Use the "Prompt as Copy" format.
- **Quote tweets:** React to travel frustration tweets with الزبدة plug.
- **Spaces:** "وش أفضل وجهة للعائلة السعودية؟" — monthly live discussion.
- **Best posting times:** Thu 9–11 PM, Fri 1–3 PM (Saudi engagement peaks).

### Snapchat
- **Snap Ads:** 6–10s max. Full-screen vertical. Quick wizard demo.
- **Filters:** "أنا مخطط رحلتي بالزبدة ✈️" — location-aware at airports.
- **Story Ads:** Behind-the-scenes of الزبدة team planning trips.
- **Targeting:** Heavy on 18–28 demographic. Riyadh + Jeddah geo.

### YouTube Shorts
- **Style:** Demo-heavy. Show the product working. 15–30s.
- **Hook:** "خطط رحلتك كاملة بدقيقتين — شوف كيف"
- **End screen:** Subscribe + app link.
- **SEO titles:** "تخطيط رحلة بالذكاء الاصطناعي | الزبدة"

## 17. Brand Safety & Crisis Guidelines [NEW]

### Brand Safety

- ❌ No placement next to political, religious controversy, or violence content
- ❌ No association with alcohol, gambling, or non-halal brands
- ✅ Block list: competitor brand keywords, controversial topics
- ✅ Inventory filter: Standard + Limited on all platforms

### Crisis Response

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Halal badge inaccuracy reported | Pause halal-focused ads (C2). Issue correction on social. Fix in app. Resume. |
| App outage during campaign | Pause all conversion ads (E-series). Run awareness only. Post "نشتغل على المشكلة" story. |
| Negative viral post about الزبدة | Do NOT engage in comments. Respond with official account calmly. Prepare counter-UGC. |
| Cultural sensitivity issue in ad | Pull immediately. Apologize simply. No excuses. Replace creative within 24h. |
